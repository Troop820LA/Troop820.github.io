5a.

Explain the importance of the buddy system as it relates to your personal safety on outings and in your neighborhood

They have to use it on an outing whenever we do one to complete the requirment.

Importance: It keeps ya safe if something goes wrong, if something goes wrong for one, the other can go get help.

5b.

Describe what to do if ya become lost on a hike or campout.

Stay still. If ya have wistle, blow 3 times, hope someone finds ya. If you have a compass, head north.
5c.

explain the rules of safe hiking, both on the highway and cross-country, during the day and at night.

During the night, wear reflective clothing and have flashlight and use buddy system. Let others know when and where you will be during this time if something goes wrong.
